About InstaDash

InstaDash is a purpose-built Reporting and Analytics Module for the HoneyComb e-commerce ecosystem. Whether you're managing products or tracking sales, it gives you the clarity you need to make faster decisions.

Key Features

  • insights Real-time sales & inventory dashboards
  • bar_chart Clean chart visualizations (bar, line, pie)
  • filter_alt Advanced filtering & search
  • vpn_key Secure login with HoneyComb ID
  • supervisor_account Role-based access (Owner, Admin, Manager, Auditor)
  • print Time-filtered export & print options

Modules We Cover

Sales Reports

Monitor trends, volumes, and monthly performance. Drill down into daily sales, identify best-selling products, and compare periods to optimize your business strategy.

Inventory Management

Track stock status in real time, receive low stock alerts, and analyze product movement. Prevent stockouts and overstocking with predictive insights and reorder suggestions.

Customer Insights

Understand customer behavior patterns, identify frequent buyers, and track loyalty. Segment your audience for targeted marketing and improve retention with actionable analytics.

Audit Logs

Admins can monitor system usage for transparency. Track user actions, changes, and access history to ensure compliance and accountability across your organization.

Courier Data

Analyze delivery status, delays, and courier performance summaries. Identify bottlenecks, improve delivery times, and enhance customer satisfaction with real-time logistics data.

Expense Tracking

Record, categorize, and analyze business expenses. Generate expense reports, monitor spending trends, and keep your finances under control with detailed breakdowns.

Marketing Analytics

Track campaign performance, conversion rates, and ROI. Visualize marketing funnels and optimize your strategies for better reach and engagement.
