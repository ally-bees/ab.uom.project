Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2025

Thank you for using Innosoft. Your privacy is important to us.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use our services:

  • First Name

  • Username
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Date of birth
  • Address, City, Country
  • Other profile-related information

In addition to the information you provide directly, we may automatically collect certain technical information about your device, browser type, operating system, IP address, and usage patterns when you interact with our services. This helps us improve our platform and provide a better user experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to authenticate your identity, manage your profile, improve services, and communicate updates. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information.

Your information may also be used to personalize your experience, respond to your inquiries, and send you important notifications about your account or changes to our services. We may use aggregated, non-identifiable data for analytics and research purposes to enhance our offerings.

3. How We Protect Your Information

We take security measures like encryption and access controls. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure.

We regularly review our data collection, storage, and processing practices to guard against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal information. Access to your personal data is restricted to employees, contractors, and agents who need to know that information in order to operate, develop, or improve our services.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties except as necessary to provide our services, comply with the law, or protect our rights. In some cases, we may share information with trusted partners who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or servicing you, provided those parties agree to keep this information confidential.

We may also disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or a government agency).

5. Your Rights

You may access, update, or delete your information by contacting us at InnosoftContact@innosoft.com.

You have the right to request a copy of the personal information we hold about you, to correct any inaccuracies, and to withdraw your consent to our processing of your data at any time, subject to legal or contractual restrictions. To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided above.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. When your information is no longer needed, we will securely delete or anonymize it.

7. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and maintained on servers located outside your country of residence. We take appropriate steps to ensure that your data is treated securely and in accordance with this Privacy Policy, regardless of where it is processed.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information from our records.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy and will post any changes on this page with a new date.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information. Your continued use of our services after any changes to this policy will constitute your acknowledgment of the changes and your consent to abide and be bound by the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at InnosoftContact@innosoft.com. We are committed to working with you to obtain a fair resolution of any complaint or concern about privacy.
